Our Philosophy

According to evidence-based science, ever since March of 2020 Americans aged 2-115 have been stressed the fuck out. While the causes are likely linked to the long term traumas of inequality and systemic injustice that have only been exacerbated by the current global pandemic, here at Wellness Activity Wellness we are confident that there is a solution: wellness activities.

At Wellness Activity Wellness, we take a pro-wellness approach to our wellness programming, incorporating the three principles of wellness to better equip future-leaders for enacting a growth mindset of success.

Professionals are ready to assist you today!

Radical Complaining

The world actually sucks so bad, and if you’re miserable that’s probably why. We’ll let you complain about it for a while before telling you that the problem is actually the way you think about the world.

Empowering Ignorance

We recognize with assignments and rent due soon, so soon, participating in a wellness activity can actually add to the amount of stress in your life. That’s why in addition to offering synchronous classes, we also offer an asynchronous learning option where attendance is not only not mandatory, it’s discouraged! Benefit from corporate goodwill by vaguely acknowledging that experts are working for you while you take a nap or finish a problem set.
