Our Philosophy

According to evidence-based science, ever since March of 2020 Americans aged 2-115 have been stressed the fuck out. While the causes are likely linked to the long term traumas of inequality and systemic injustice that have only been exacerbated by the current global pandemic, here at Wellness Activity Wellness we are confident that there is a solution: wellness activities.

At Wellness Activity Wellness, we take a pro-wellness approach to our wellness programming, incorporating the three principles of wellness to better equip future-leaders for enacting a growth mindset of success.

Professionals are ready to assist you today!

Radical Complaining

The world actually sucks so bad, and if you’re miserable that’s probably why. Instead of positive thinking, we encourage and empower future leaders to learn from recent scientific advances in the field of negative psychology: the power of negative thinking. Using our proven methods, you'll be feeling empathy for the poor and huddled masses in no time!

Empowering Ignorance

We recognize with assignments and rent due soon, so soon, participating in a wellness activity can actually add to the amount of stress in your life. That’s why we offer an asynchronous learning option where attendance is not only not mandatory, it’s discouraged! Benefit from corporate goodwill by vaguely acknowledging that experts are working for you while you take a nap or finish a problem set.

About Us

WAWellness is Lily "LEF" Friedberg and Tommy "Jhomathy" Schneider. We're doing this for 3 reasons:

  • Reason 1

    To challenge the way corporate/administrative culture approaches mental health as an individualistic problem divorced from systematic oppression and injustice.

  • Reason 2

    To create a radical, artistic, and humorous space for friends and students to build solidarity and (optionally) do fun activities together.

  • Reason 3

    To direct funding toward Black Voters Matter and the New Georgia Project. These two organizations share a short term goal of winning the Jan 5 Senate elections in Georgia and a long term goal of combating the systematic disenfranchisement of Black Americans in Georgia and advancing a more equitable policy agenda.

Dante’s Nine Circles of Wellness

Wellness exists along many dimensions. For example, one person could have depression but not COVID, while another could have COVID but not depression. Using methods from linear algebra and machine learning, we have determined that total wellness can be parametrized along Dante’s nine circles. Join us for a discussion of hell and how to feel good when you’re in it.

Emotional Intelligence Seminar

Now more than ever, it’s important to maintain awareness of the existence of other consciousnesses. We will practice mindlessness meditation, spacing out and other dissociative skills to improve our distress tolerance, and other useful interpersonal skills, such as interpreting microexpressions in frozen zoom frames and modulating tone by way of emojis.
